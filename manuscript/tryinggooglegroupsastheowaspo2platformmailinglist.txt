##  Trying Google Groups as the OWASP O2 Platform mailing list 

This is something that some OWASP project leaders have talked/tested in the past, so to try it I moved the [current mailman-based O2 list](https://lists.owasp.org/listinfo/owasp-o2-platform) to the new[ Google Groups](https://groups.google.com/) (which look much better than a couple years ago)

My first step was to go to the [mailman Subscribers page](https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/roster/owasp-o2-platform), get the 57 current subscribers:
